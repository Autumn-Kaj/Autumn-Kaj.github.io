Hi! I'm Autumn.

Here's a little bit about me.

Western Washington University Graduate

I am a graduate of Western Washington University, which is situated between the beautiful shadow of Mount Baker and the cold Pacific Ocean. Shortly after joining the University in the fall of 2016, I fell in love with the school and the town of Bellingham. I graduated in 2021, and hold a Bachelor's of Science in Industrial design, with a Minor in User Experience.

WWU Industrial Designer

Western's Industrial Design Program was an extremely competitive, yet collaborative environment where students were encouraged to learn by doing hands-on design work. I found myself in a senior class of only 14, and we were constantly hard at work proving ourselves to our peers and professors. Never had I ever been surrounded by so many talented people, it was inspiring to be in the Senior Studio with them. As a designer, I am inspired by my environment, the people who surround me, and my background every day.

Handmade Goods

One of the most rewarding projects to work on at Western was the Remade project. This project took place during Sophomore year, where the class put on a Show and Sale at Ideal, in Bellingham, Washington. This assignment introduced me to my other passion in small business and handmade goods. I now own an Etsy shop with over 287 sales to customers around the world.

Sketching

In my free time I enjoy sketching almost anything. From products to landscapes, to characters or animals: I always have a pen in hand to render my ideas.
