Mus

Pacific Northwest inspired clothing brand.

Welcome to Mus Clothing Co.

Mus's retail store design is inspired by a combination of traditional log cabins and the fresh angles of modern architecture. Materials were chosen based off of their natural, and warm attributes. The space needed to be inviting, bright, and warm, and these materials reflect this.

Clothing at its roots.

Mus clothing is hand-crafted from natural materials, and takes inspiration from its origins. Warm, naturally occurring colors and textures are a part of this brand's identity.
